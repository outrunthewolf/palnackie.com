Dumfries & Galloway Life — Cafe of the Year 2021

Flounders Community Cafe

Award-winning coffee, cake, breakfasts and lunches — conveniently located right next to the village shop.

Palnackie's award-winning community cafe will see you right for coffee, hot chocolate, cake, breakfasts and lunches. It's very conveniently located next to the village shop.

We can get very busy, so if you want to be guaranteed a table for you and your companions, it's always good to reserve in advance. On a nice warm day, there are additional outdoor tables for you.

Please check our Facebook page for last-minute changes in opening times.

Private Hire

The cafe is also available for private hire outside of its normal opening times. It seats 22 people, making it ideal for:

  • Book clubs
  • Knit & natter groups
  • Small training sessions
  • Cookery lessons
  • Get-togethers and celebrations

Please inquire for availability. If 22 is too small, the Village Hall can be hired with a capacity of 120.
