Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisation

Buittle Quest SCIO

The charity set up to benefit the people of Buittle Parish — and owners of our village hall and the north side of the port.

Buittle Quest was set up to benefit the people of Buittle Parish and members of the public, groups and organisations visiting Buittle Parish. Buittle Parish is the area administered by Buittle Parish Community Council.

The Buittle Parish Boundary Map can be found here: info.dumgal.gov.uk.

Buittle Quest owns our village hall and the north side of our port.
