Emergencies & Useful Numbers

Key emergency contacts and useful numbers for Palnackie and the wider Dumfries & Galloway area.

In an emergency, always call 999 first.

For non-urgent police matters, call 101. For non-urgent medical advice, call NHS 24 on 111.
