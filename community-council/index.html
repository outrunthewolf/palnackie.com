Community

Buittle Parish Community Council

Our community council is consulted by law on issues that matter to our community — and it has to listen to us on issues important to us.

Our community council receives and can comment on planning and licensing applications relevant to the Buittle Parish Community Council boundary. Palnackie sits within the area of Buittle Parish.

The meeting schedule, contact details and the Buittle Parish Boundary Map can be found on the Dumfries and Galloway Council website:

Buittle Parish Community Council — Dumfries & Galloway Council

Minutes of recent Buittle Parish Community Council meetings are available in the village shop.

Community councils are voluntary organisations run by local residents — aged 16 and above — who care about their community. If you'd like to get involved, contact the council via the link above.
