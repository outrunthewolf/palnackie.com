Available to hire

Palnackie Village Hall

Planning a party, a class or an event? Our village hall seats 120 people with a large lawned rear garden — available for a wide range of occasions.

Do you want to organise a party, a class or a training event? The village hall caters for a variety of events: weddings, birthday parties, children's parties, barbecue events, garden parties, dance classes and dances, painting classes, aerobics, yoga, music events, corporate events and more. The hall has a large lawned rear garden with a medium-size patio.

Capacity

Palnackie Village Hall has a maximum capacity of 120 people and can comfortably seat 100 people at tables. Chairs and folding tables, including table covers, are available in the hall.

If this is too big for you, Flounders Community Cafe can also be hired — it has a capacity of 22.

Facilities

Access to the hall is via a lobby. Off the main hall there is a kitchen, toilets and two storage rooms. The kitchen has a service hatch, cooker, oven and fridge, crockery and cutlery (you will need to bring your own cooking pots). Access to the garden is from the hall or via a side gate.

Disabled Access

  • Ramp to the front of the hall
  • Disabled toilet
  • Level access to the garden via the side gate
  • Access from the hall to the garden is through double doors onto a paved patio, with a step over a 3" (8cm) door sill
