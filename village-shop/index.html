Community-owned

The Village Shop

Run for our community by our community — with a Post Office, local produce and a defibrillator outside the door.

Our village shop sells a wide range of basic provisions, alcoholic drinks and a growing range of local and fresh produce, baked goods and gift items. Come and have a look! Please check our Facebook page to see what else is on offer today or later in the week.

The post office in the shop is open on Mondays and Fridays from 9am to 10am.

Just outside the shop we have the village post box and our defibrillator station.

Please check our Facebook page for last-minute changes in opening times.
