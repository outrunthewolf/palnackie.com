Places to Stay

Holly Cottage is within the village itself. Kippford View Holiday Park and Kirkennan Estate Holiday Cottages are both within view of Palnackie. Buittle Castle is a short drive away.

Holly Cottage

2 Yettan Terrace, Palnackie, DG7 1PG

An attractive cottage located in a quiet corner of Palnackie. Self-catering and sleeps 6 people (and 1 dog) in 3 bedrooms.

Kirkennan Estate Holiday Cottages

Palnackie, DG7 1PE

Three self-catering cottages set among formal lawns, a walled garden, and woodland and fields on the banks of the River Urr. The Mews sleeps 5 in 2 bedrooms, Kirkennan Lodge sleeps 7 in 4 bedrooms, and Woodsedge sleeps 5 in 3 bedrooms. All cottages welcome up to 3 well-behaved dogs. EV charging points available.

DG7 1PA (between Palnackie and Dalbeattie)
